Interact with your audience

Poll App give you an easy way to interact with your audience during presentations, courses, etc.

Easy to share & fully interactive

Simply launch a poll room and share the four digit ID with your audience. Once logged in your room the users will be waiting for your questions. You can send each question at the right moment during your presentation, course, etc.

Single / Multiple choice

We support questions with single and multiple choice.

Timmer

You can setup a different delay for your questions so that your audience will have the right time to answer each ones.

Show the results

You will have live results and your audience will have them too at the end of the question.

Stats

Show nice stats about the response rate of each answers.

Directly see the right answers

The right answers are shown with a green background. In this way users can easily see if they have answered correctly.

Identify your answer

The answers of the user are marked with an arrow on the left.

Manage your polls

Prepare your questions and be ready for your presentation, course, etc.

Group your questions

You can create groups of questions. So you can sort them by subject, presentation, course or in any ways that fits you best.

Easy edit

You can add and delete questions and answers with a simple click.

Audience

You will have at every moment the number of users connected to your poll room.
